🧮 Tile Formula Configuration

Customize how each dashboard tile value is calculated. Changes take effect immediately after saving.

📖 Available formula variables (click to expand)
income — Income transactions (salary, rental…)
regularExpenses — Non-investment expense transactions (after adjustments)
expenseAdj — Statement-adjustment / refund credits
emiTotal — Loan EMI outflow this month
lumpSumInvestmentOutflow — One-time investment outflow for selected month
sipTotal — Monthly SIP outflow from manual investments
savingsOutflow — Monthly liquid savings contribution outflow
emergencyOutflow — Monthly emergency fund allocation outflow
retirementOutflow — Monthly retirement contribution outflow
investmentTx — Investment-tagged expense transactions
prevBalance — Cash carry-over from previous months
allExpenseTx — All expense transactions (gross, inc. investments)
portfolioValue — Investment portfolio projected value
principalValue — Investment cost / principal
totalAssets — All tracked assets value
emergencyFund — Emergency fund balance
netWorth — Net-worth eligible assets – liabilities
totalLiabilities — Outstanding liabilities total
liquidSavings — Liquid savings products total
retirementCorpus — Retirement products total corpus
insurancePremium — Total active insurance premium
Math.max / Math.min — Math helpers are allowed
